About

Vi Snake is similar to the snake game on old Nokia phones. The h, j, k, l keys are used to change directions, like in Vi/Vim text editor.

The game is open source, and distributed under the MIT license. Source code is available on github.

Upon playing your score will be sent to the server. Your last score and maximum score is stored in a session cookie. It is automatically deleted when you close your browser, or after 31 days.

If you get a high enough score, you are prompted to enter your name to get on the highscore list. The highscores (score, username and date) are stored on a database on the server. Should you need to remove or export your data from the highscore list, please contact me by opening an issue on github, or message me on twitter or linked-in.
